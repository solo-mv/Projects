Banana Cream Pie

Ingredients

  1. ¾ cup white sugar
  2. ⅓ cup all-purpose flour
  3. ¼ teaspoon salt
  4. 2 cups milk
  5. 2 tablespoons butter
  6. 1 ¼ teaspoons vanilla extract
  7. 1 (9 inch) baked pastry shell, cooled

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    Combine sugar, flour, and salt in a saucepan. Gradually stir in milk. Cook, stirring constantly, over medium heat until mixture boils and thickens. Continue to cook and stir for 2 more minutes, then remove from the heat.

  3. Step 3

    Place egg yolks in a medium bowl; whisk in a small amount of the hot milk mixture until smooth, then gradually whisk egg yolk mixture into the saucepan. Cook over medium-low heat, stirring constantly, for 2 more minutes. Remove from heat and stir in butter and vanilla.

  4. Step 4

    Fill pastry shell with sliced bananas; pour pudding mixture on top to cover.

  5. Step 5

    Bake in the preheated oven until filling sets, 12 to 15 minutes. Chill pie for 1 hour before serving.
